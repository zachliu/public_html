This project requires estimating light source directions, surface normals, albedos and depth (based on surface normals) from a series of chrome sphere images. Then the images can be relit from any direction (by Relight.pde).

Notes

  • All tasks are fulfilled as required.
  • Trick for handling shadows is used: weight each equation by the pixel brightness.
  • Normals are solved based on lecture notes: inverse of ST*S.
  • Albedos are solved by the inverse of Sn and then times I(IR, IG, and IB)
