Notes

  • The Lucas-Kanade algorithm works but it will take a long time to finish especially when the initial translations are not accurate enough.
  • When dealing with images larger than 1024x768, Processing and NetBeans tend to stop with "out of memory" message under Windows system. However this will not happen under Ubuntu linux.
  • A tripod was used in Test II and III. But not in Test IV.
  • A simple brightness adjustment routine was integrated with blending during the final stage - make panorama. In this routine, every image will be adjusted comparing with previous image only on their overlapped part.
  • Since there was no tripod involved in Test IV, I guess it's one of the reasons that it didn't look so good.
